What is Indivisible CA-14?

We are citizens of the California 14th Congressional District who oppose President Trump's ill-conceived and poorly implemented agenda.

We come from a spectrum of political views and backgrounds, but are unified by our opposition to non-inclusive and undemocratic parts of this agenda ranging from the assaults on women and people of color to the attacks on our independent judiciary.

We will use inclusion, respect, fairness, and truth to push our representatives towards a better country and future.
